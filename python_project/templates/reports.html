Report Information

The salon management system provides various reports to help you analyze your business performance:

  • Daily Report: Shows all transactions for a specific day, including customer details, services, and staff payouts.
  • Monthly Report: Provides a monthly summary of revenue and payouts for each staff member.
  • Performance Report: Analyzes staff performance metrics such as customer count, average revenue per customer, and total revenue.
  • Payout Report: Calculates payouts for each staff member based on their commission rates and transaction amounts.

All reports can be exported as CSV files for further analysis or record-keeping.
